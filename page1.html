About Us

Christoph Octavianthe Putra

Mr Christoph is one of the most established Springfield staff, guiding a wide variety of creative dreamers from Grade 9 to Grade 12. He assist students in establishing their portfolios in order to prepare them for the future. He emphasized on the fact that artistic intrepertations contain powerful messages. 

Content

During the first semester, the students are tasked to follow the teachings of the teacher and apply what they learn into a piece of paper. Afterwards, the class would discuss on the definitions and/or emotions through speech or presentation.

During the second semester, the students are given the ability to develop their portfolio by the techniques learned during the lectures or present their own art style by experience. They are also given time to add flavor text alongside their pictures in order to convey the message of their artwork.
